Refund Policy

How do I return my order?

We are committed to bringing you the freshest supply. However, grain, like other grains is perishable. We consider returned goods, compromised in freshness. So at Jaipur Chakki, we do not have a return policy, but please feel free to write to us at info@jaipurchakki.com.in and we promise whatever we can do to resolve the issue.

Received the wrong shipment, What do I do now?

To err is human; despite our best effort to give you only the best whole grain product, maybe something went wrong. Please feel free to shoot an email to We shall make up in the best way possible, or else refund your money right back to you.

How do you Refund my money?
Jaipur Chakki will process the refund based on your chosen mode of payment. This typically takes about 5-7 working days.
